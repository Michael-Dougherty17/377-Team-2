About Our Project

Students at UMD have a hard time getting used to the bus schedule when they first arrive. The bus apps offered to students are not intuitive and do not provide users with everything they need like trip length and arrival times. We hope to provide students with a simple and easy solution to get where they need to be on time. This app will help them manage schedules better and know when they will arrive at their destinations.The user would enter their starting and ending destinations into the app. Next, using data from the API, the app will show which bus routes are available to them and when those buses will arrive at their current stop. Once a bus has been selected, the app will display how long it will take to get to their destination. We will use the UMD.io API to access bus route information. This API holds the data like bus stops, route times, and bus numbers, which are all necessary to solve the information problem.
